SubTrack

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

SubTrack ("the app", "we", "us") is a subscription tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights as a user.

SubTrack is operated by Ömer Faruk Göze ("Controller") and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

By using SubTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password — stored as a hashed value; we never see your plain-text password

2.2 Subscription Data (User-Entered)

  • Subscription name, amount, currency, billing cycle, category, renewal date
  • Notes and tags you add to subscriptions

This data is entered by you and stored in your account.

2.3 Bank Account Data Premium

If you choose to connect your bank account, we collect:

  • Bank account metadata (account name, institution name, last 4 digits of IBAN)
  • Transaction history from the last 90 days (amount, description, date, category)
  • Detected recurring subscriptions derived from transaction analysis
Bank connectivity is optional. SubTrack works fully without it. Bank data is processed via Tink (a Visa-licensed, PSD2-regulated Account Information Service Provider). We never see or store your bank login credentials.

2.4 Purchase Data Premium

When you subscribe to SubTrack Premium, your purchase is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We receive a purchase receipt to verify your subscription status. We do not store credit card or payment method details.

2.5 Notification Preferences

  • Push notification settings (enabled/disabled, reminder timing)
  • Notification history (which reminders were sent)

2.6 Calendar Data

If you grant calendar permission, SubTrack creates calendar events for subscription renewal reminders. We access your calendar only to create and manage these events. We do not read, store, or transmit your existing calendar content.

2.7 Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID / fingerprint login, biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (iOS Secure Enclave / Android Keystore). Biometric data is never transmitted to SubTrack servers.

2.8 Technical Data

  • Device type and OS version (for crash diagnostics and compatibility)
  • App version

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the subscription tracking service
  • To automatically detect recurring subscriptions from your bank transactions (if connected)
  • To send renewal reminders via push notifications and/or calendar events
  • To verify your SubTrack Premium subscription status
  • To enable biometric login on your device
  • To allow you to export your own data
  • To maintain the security and integrity of your account
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

7. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit: All communication uses TLS 1.2+
  • Encryption at rest: All data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest
  • Row Level Security: Database policies ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Token security: Tink API credentials stored only in server-side Edge Function environment — never in the client app
  • Biometric data: Processed exclusively by your device OS; never transmitted
  • No raw credential storage: We never store your bank login credentials

In the event of a data breach involving bank data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.
